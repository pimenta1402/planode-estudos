## 汳｡ DICAS IMPORTANTES

### **Present Perfect vs Present Perfect Continuous:**
- **Since 2018** = Present Perfect Continuous (duraﾃｧﾃ｣o)
- **Still learning** = Present Continuous (aﾃｧﾃ｣o em progresso)
- **Just arrived** = Present Perfect Simple (aﾃｧﾃ｣o recente)

### **Future Forms:**
- **Evidence** (baby trying to walk) = "going to"
- **Schedules** (store hours) = Present Simple
- **Arranjos pessoais** = Present Continuous

### **Question Tags:**
- **Everyone/Everybody** = "they" (plural)
- **Let's** = "shall we"
- **Let's not** = "shall we" (mesmo sendo negativo)

### **First Conditional:**
- **If + present simple, will + infinitive**
- Use para situaﾃｧﾃｵes reais e possﾃｭveis
- Pode usar contraﾃｧﾃｵes
